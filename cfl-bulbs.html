Can you throw away CFL bulbs?

Learn whether CFL bulbs can go in the trash, when to recycle it, and what safer alternatives may be better.

Quick answer

Usually should not go in regular trash because of mercury content.

CategoryLight Bulbs
Recycle?Usually yes
Donate?No
Safer optionhazardous waste collection
Main concernMercury content can require special disposal.

Best next step

In many situations, the better option is hazardous waste collection rather than immediate trash disposal. Final rules can still vary by local program.

Why this answer can vary

Disposal guidance often changes based on local waste programs, contamination, whether the item still works, and whether the item contains mixed materials or hazardous components.

Frequently asked questions

Can you throw away CFL bulbs?

Usually should not go in regular trash because of mercury content.

Is recycling better for CFL bulbs?

Usually yes. Recycling is often worth checking first when a program accepts the item.

Can you donate CFL bulbs?

No. Donation depends mostly on whether the item still works or is still usable.
