Can you throw away button batteries?

Learn whether button batteries can go in the trash, when to recycle it, and what safer alternatives may be better.

Quick answer

Use battery recycling options and keep them away from children.

CategoryBatteries
Recycle?Usually yes
Donate?No
Safer optionbattery recycling site
Main concernSwallowing risk for children and special disposal concerns.

Best next step

In many situations, the better option is battery recycling site rather than immediate trash disposal. Final rules can still vary by local program.

Why this answer can vary

Disposal guidance often changes based on local waste programs, contamination, whether the item still works, and whether the item contains mixed materials or hazardous components.

Frequently asked questions

Can you throw away button batteries?

Use battery recycling options and keep them away from children.

Is recycling better for button batteries?

Usually yes. Recycling is often worth checking first when a program accepts the item.

Can you donate button batteries?

No. Donation depends mostly on whether the item still works or is still usable.
