📺 Electronics

Electronics often contain materials that make recycling a better choice than trash disposal. Working electronics may also be good candidates for donation or resale.

Frequently asked questions

Can electronics items always go in the trash?

Not always. The best disposal method depends on the exact item, its condition, and local rules.

Should electronics items be recycled when possible?

Recycling is often the better option when a local program accepts the item.

Do local rules matter for electronics disposal?

Yes. Disposal programs can vary by city, county, and state.
